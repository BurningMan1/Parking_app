Why is BakuParking so specaial?

BakuParking is the one and only mobile parking app in Azerbaijan.

Do the right choice, save your time.

There are a lot of parking areas in Baku, but none of them are working with a centralized system. Most of the individual parking systems in Baku are located in the central parts of the city, leaving other areas to be free of any systematic parking. Which means there is no information about possible parking spaces around the city. Our application will merge all of the parking spots in the city into a system, making it easier for people to find empty spaces. With this, it will also help solving traffic problems around city. The prices of the parking spots will vary depending on the location of the spot. For example, in the center of the City, prices will be higher compared to suburban locations.

Escape Traffic

Traffic in Baku is chaotic, and parking is a big factor in creating this chaos. This system will cover whole of the city, meaning it will also bring order to parking in streets. People will be able to get permanent parking spaces close to their living areas. The search for parking spaces will be eradicated, which will decrease waste of time on parking.
